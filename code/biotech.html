Overview of Biotechnology & Genetics

Introduction to Biotechnology & Genetics Careers :


Biotechnology and genetics are rapidly evolving fields that combine biology, chemistry, and technology to develop innovative solutions in medicine, agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and environmental science. These fields involve the manipulation of biological systems and genetic material to improve human health, increase crop production, and develop sustainable solutions for the environment.
