Career Opportunities

1. Film Director

    o Description: Oversees creative direction of films/shows.
    o Responsibilities: Storytelling, directing actors, managing production teams.
    o Industries: Film, OTT, Ad Films.


2. Video Editor

    o Description: Assembles raw footage into polished products.
    o Responsibilities: Editing, visual effects, syncing sound.
    o Industries: Post-production houses, YouTube channels.


3. Cinematographer

    o Description: Manages the visual look and camera work.
    o Responsibilities: Lighting, camera movement, shot composition.
    o Industries: Feature Films, Documentaries, TV Shows.


4. Producer

    o Description: Manages finances, schedules, and overall production.
    o Responsibilities: Budgeting, hiring, planning logistics.
    o Industries: Studios, Advertising, OTT Production Houses.
