Overview of Forensic Science

Introduction to Forensic Science as a Career Field :


Forensic science is a multidisciplinary field that combines biology, chemistry, physics, and law to analyze crime scene evidence and support legal investigations. Forensic experts work with law enforcement, government agencies, private firms, and cybersecurity teams to solve crimes, identify fraud, and ensure justice.
