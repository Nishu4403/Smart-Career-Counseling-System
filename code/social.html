Career Opportunities

1. Social Worker

    o Description: Supports individuals and communities in distress.
    o Responsibilities: Casework, advocacy, counseling.
    o Employers: NGOs, hospitals, schools.


2. Public Administrator

    o Description: Manages public sector organizations and policies.
    o Responsibilities: Planning, coordination, implementation of public programs.
    o Employers: Government, Municipal Corporations.


3. Policy Analyst

    o Description: Evaluates and formulates public policies.
    o Responsibilities: Research, data analysis, recommendations.
    o Employers: Think tanks, government departments.


4. NGO Project Coordinator

    o Description: Plans and executes nonprofit programs.
    o Responsibilities: Budgeting, team management, reporting.
    o Employers: National & international NGOs.


5. Community Development Officer

    o Description: Works for the upliftment of underprivileged communities.
    o Responsibilities: Community outreach, education, health and livelihood support.
    o Employers: Government departments, NGOs, CSR units.


Skills Required

Key Skills for Social Work and Public Administration:


    1. Empathy and compassion
    2. Communication and interpersonal skills
    3. Knowledge of social laws and public policy
    4. Analytical and problem-solving skills
    5. Leadership and team coordination
    6. Administrative and organizational skills
    7. Multitasking and crisis management
