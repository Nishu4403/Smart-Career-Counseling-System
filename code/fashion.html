Career Opportunities

1. Fashion Designer

    o Description: Designs clothing lines and collections based on trends and themes.
    o Responsibilities: Concept creation, fabric selection, prototyping, and runway shows.
    o Industries: Apparel, luxury brands, retail.


2. Fashion Stylist

    o Description: Coordinates outfits and styles for celebrities, photo shoots, or TV shows.
    o Responsibilities: Styling, fitting, accessorizing, trend analysis.
    o Industries: Media, film, fashion magazines.


3. Textile Designer

    o Description: Creates patterns, weaves, and prints for fabrics.
    o Responsibilities: Designing repeat patterns, fabric testing, collaborating with designers.
    o Industries: Textile mills, home decor, fashion brands.


4. Fashion Blogger/Influencer

    o Description: Shares fashion tips and trends online through blogs, Instagram, or YouTube.
    o Responsibilities: Content creation, brand collaboration, personal styling.
    o Industries: Social media, marketing, digital branding.
