Journalism involves the gathering, assessing, creating, and presenting news and information to the public through various formats, such as print, digital, and broadcast media. Journalists research, write, and report on current events and issues, aiming to inform and engage the public.

Mass Communication refers to the process of delivering information and entertainment to large audiences via different forms of media. It encompasses journalism, advertising, public relations, and entertainment media, with the goal of influencing public opinion, spreading awareness, and fostering societal engagement.

Career Opportunities

1. Journalist/Reporter

    o Description: Journalists gather, investigate, and report news to the public. They work across various media platforms like newspapers, magazines, television, and online outlets.

    o Key Responsibilities: Investigating stories, interviewing sources, writing articles, covering events, and ensuring accurate reporting.

    o Industries: Media outlets, news agencies, freelance, online publications.


2. Editor

    o Description: Editors manage and oversee the content being published, ensuring that articles are well-written, accurate, and engaging.

    o Key Responsibilities: Editing content, overseeing writers, setting editorial standards, and ensuring deadlines are met.

    o Industries: Newspapers, magazines, digital media, publishing companies.


3. Broadcast Journalist

    o Description: Broadcast journalists work in radio or television, gathering and presenting news and reports.

    o Key Responsibilities: Writing scripts, presenting news on air, reporting live from events, editing video/audio, and managing broadcast segments.

    o Industries: TV networks, radio stations, online media, freelance.


4. Media Researcher

    o Description: Media researchers gather and analyze data on audience preferences, market trends, and media consumption.

    o Key Responsibilities: Conducting research, analyzing data, producing reports, and helping shape media content strategies.

    o Industries: Media agencies, research firms, TV networks.
