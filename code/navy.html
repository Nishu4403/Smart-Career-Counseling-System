Career Opportunities

1. **Deck Officer / Navigation Officer**

    o Responsible for ship navigation, cargo handling, and onboard communication.
    o Employers: Shipping corporations, private shipping companies.


2. **Marine Engineer**

    o Manages engine room, propulsion systems, and onboard machinery.
    o Employers: Shipping companies, cruise lines, oil tankers.


3. **Electro Technical Officer (ETO)**

    o Maintains all electrical and electronic systems on the ship.
    o Employers: Cargo ships, container ships, cruise ships.


4. **Chief Engineer / Captain**

    o Senior-most roles in engine and deck departments, respectively.
    o Employers: Global shipping lines, oil and gas fleets.


5. **Port and Harbour Manager**

    o Manages operations at ports and harbors including loading/unloading, logistics.
    o Employers: Port authorities, logistics firms.
