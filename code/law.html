Career Opportunities

1. Litigation Lawyer

    o Role: Represents clients in court, prepares legal documents, and argues cases before judges.
    o Specializations: Criminal law, civil law, family law, etc.
    o Career Path: Start as a junior lawyer in a law firm, then move up to senior positions or start an independent practice.


2. Corporate Lawyer

    o Role: Handles legal matters for corporations, including mergers, acquisitions, contracts, and compliance.
    o Specializations: Corporate law, business law, intellectual property law.
    o Career Path: Work with top law firms or as in-house counsel in large corporations.


3. Human Rights Lawyer

    o Role: Focuses on advocating for the protection and promotion of human rights at local, national, or international levels.
    o Specializations: Human rights, social justice, international law.
    o Career Path: Work for international organizations, government bodies, or human rights NGOs.


4. Legal Consultant

    o Role: Provides expert legal advice to companies, institutions, or individuals, but doesn't typically represent clients in court.
    o Specializations: Contract law, employment law, international law, corporate governance.
    o Career Path: Work independently, with consulting firms, or as a freelancer.
