Exam/Program Description Departments
UPSC Civil Services Exam (IAS/IPS/IFS) National-level exam to recruit top bureaucrats for Indian Administrative, Police, and Foreign Services. Administration, Police, External Affairs
SSC CGL/CHSL Staff Selection Commission exams for recruiting to clerical, assistant, and officer roles in ministries and departments. Income Tax, Excise, Railways, Audit, Central Secretariat
State PSC Exams State-level exams for administrative roles like Deputy Collector, Tahsildar, etc. State Administration, Revenue, Rural Development
Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) Recruitment exams for various technical and non-technical roles in Indian Railways. Operations, Engineering, Accounts, Administration
Defense Services (NDA/CDS/AFCAT) Exams to join Indian Army, Navy, and Air Force as officers. Defense, Logistics, Engineering, Intelligence

Career Opportunities

1. **Indian Administrative Services (IAS) Officer**

    o Role: Responsible for policy implementation and public administration.
    o Employers: Government of India, State Governments.


2. **Indian Police Services (IPS) Officer**

    o Role: Maintains law and order and manages police departments.
    o Employers: State Police Departments, Central Agencies like CBI, NIA.


3. **Revenue Officer / Income Tax Officer**

    o Role: Manages taxation, revenue collection, and financial audits.
    o Employers: Income Tax Department, Central Board of Direct Taxes.


4. **Defense Officer (Army, Navy, Air Force)**

    o Role: Protects national security and manages defense operations.
    o Employers: Ministry of Defence.


5. **Public Sector Bank Officer**

    o Role: Manages banking operations and public financial services.
    o Employers: SBI, PNB, RBI, and other nationalized banks.
